Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Your first visit begins with a complimentary consultation where we discuss your aesthetic goals, review your medical history, and perform a thorough skin analysis. We'll then create a personalized treatment plan and answer all of your questions before proceeding with any treatment.
Botox results typically last 3-4 months. With regular maintenance treatments, many patients find their results lasting longer over time as the muscles become trained to relax. We recommend scheduling follow-up appointments to maintain your refreshed look.
Yes, dermal fillers are FDA-approved and have an excellent safety profile when administered by qualified medical professionals. At Livia, all injectable treatments are performed by board-certified practitioners with extensive training in facial anatomy and aesthetics.
Downtime varies by treatment. Botox and fillers typically require no downtime — most patients return to normal activities immediately. Laser treatments and chemical peels may require 3-7 days of healing depending on intensity. We'll discuss specific recovery expectations during your consultation.
Yes! We want our treatments to be accessible. We accept all major credit cards and offer financing options through CareCredit and Cherry, allowing you to break your treatment cost into manageable monthly payments with competitive interest rates.
Livia is a boutique, physician-led med spa focused on natural-looking results. We never take a one-size-fits-all approach — every treatment is customized to your unique facial structure, skin type, and aesthetic goals. Our intimate setting means more personalized attention than larger clinics.
